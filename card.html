Siberian Tiger

The Siberian tiger and Bengal tiger subspecies rank among the biggest living cats.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera tigris altaica
  • Average Length: 3.3 m (11ft.)
  • Average Lifespan: 10-15 years
  • Habitat: Eastern Russia's birch forests
  • Type: Mammal
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Average weight: 100kg-350kg (220lbs-770lbs)
  • Fur Color: Orange, Black, White

The Siberian tiger is a dominant and carnivorous predator, hunting its prey by stalking it until the Siberian tiger has the opportunity to catch it off guard. Siberian tigers primarily hunt larger mammals including deer, wild boar, cattle and goats.
